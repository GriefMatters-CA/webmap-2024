Grief Gatherings 2024

Welcome to Grief Gatherings 2024.

Grief Gatherings is a series of events to increase grief literacy in our communities.

Events happen September 20 - 29, 2024, online and in-person.

This map shows the locations of events which have an in-person component. In-person events with an online option are shown with a turquoise border.

Navigate to the "Virtual Events" pane in the sidebar to view events which are online only

If you have any questions, please reach out to info@griefmatters.ca.
